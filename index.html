The Future of Chessboxing at Port Credit

Chessboxing represents the perfect synthesis of mental and physical discipline, offering students a unique opportunity to develop both strategic thinking and physical fitness in a safe, controlled environment. With established precedents in schools and colleges worldwide, and with safety as our top priority, we're excited to bring this innovative sport to our community.

Whether you're interested in competing, spectating, or just learning more about this fascinating sport, we invite you to be part of this exciting new chapter at Port Credit. Together, we can create something truly special that promotes wellness, camaraderie, and school spirit.
